Medicare Medical Center and Diagnostics is a multi-speciality Polyclinic, Medical and Diagnostic Centre.

It is one of its kind in western suburbs having Pathology, Radiology, Cardiology Consultations & Health Checkups under one roof. It is born out of a passion to pioneer and excel in the field of preventive diagnostics.

It has excellent infrastructure and reassuring environment to aid services given by dedicated medical professionals.

All the consulting rooms are spacious, air conditioned, fully equipped & decently furnished with intercom facility within a healthy atmosphere. The centers function with full-time receptionists (8 A.M. TO 8. P.M.), with telephone lines for round the clock appointments and informationto the patients.

A disciplined, trained and hardworking staff is provided for smooth functioning of the centers. The whole establishment is looked after by a team of in house highly qualified and experienced Doctors and full-time centre managers.Everything we do is governed by objectives like:

  • The welfare of our patients.
  • Ensuring Accuracy and timely delivery of reports.
  • Bring out the best in employed individuals.
